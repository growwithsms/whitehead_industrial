Product Description

Features:

  • Made in USA
  • Silver and Deming 1/2" reduced shank - with 3 flats
  • Common flute and overall length for minimal adjustment during tool changes
  • Nitride Surface treatment, that gives hard, protective layer to the drill (increases hardness without increasing brittleness), which is measurably harder than high speed steel
  • Body and clearance are black and gold surface treated for maximum lubricity
  • The 118 degree split point reduces thrust and eliminates walking at the drill point
  • Precision ground for a high degree of concentricity between shank and body diameter
  • 3" flute length, 6" length

Application:

  • Recommended for use in stainless steel and other hard metal
  • These drills are not recommended for enlarging existing holes
  • Recommended for use with 1/2" portable drill chuck
